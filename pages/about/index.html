About

To be the basic tool and service on the WWW.

Shapefly is a HTML5 web application for inserting ready-made shapes on the web, and it is also a service, lets users drawing on their own websites by importing shapefly-plugin.Our purpose of shapefly is to be a basic tool and service on the WWW.Shapefly is a newborn product, there should be bugs, shortage in some stuffs.So give us advices or bug reports if possible.
